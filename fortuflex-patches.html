Fortuflex patches

Fortuflex patches provide targeted and long-term support for your joints and help you fight joint pain. The benefit of the patches is that they act directly on the concerned area and provide gradual transfer of the active ingredients, such as glucosamine and chondroitin into skin. Thus, the patches have a long-term effect and help to relieve pain as well as to heal the affected joint.

The patches contain glucosamine and chondroitin which help to restore the cartilageous tissue and make the joint active again. They also contain fortunella extract which has antiinflammation effect and hydrates the skin. Menthol gives a cooling effect and helps to get rid of oedema.

Fortuflex patches

The patches act up to 12 hours and are easy to use. They can be used as an instant pain reliever as well as a part of therapy to restore joint health.

Apply to the damaged area and rub in. Wash your hands after use. Don’t use in case of allergic response.
