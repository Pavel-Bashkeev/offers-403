Help&Info

All our products will be delivered within 1-3 business days. The goods will be dispatched from the local warehouse within 24 hours. You will get an email with confirmation of your order. After the item is delivered, you’ll be able to get it in the post office. If the product you’ve ordered is out of stock, we’ll give you a call and let you know when it’s going to be available.

Returns

You have 30 days after receiving the goods to return your order. You should write an application and specify the reason for the return. If we accept your application, we’ll give you all the instructions on how to send the product back. If you’ve started using the product of proper quality, unfortunately, we can’t accept your return. Please contact us immediately if you have received the wrong, defective or damaged item.

Exchanges

If you’ve ordered the wrong product, just return it without opening the package and place a new order.

Refunds

We’ll contact you when we examine your item and we’ll let you know if we approve your return. If we do, the money will be refunded on your original payment method.
